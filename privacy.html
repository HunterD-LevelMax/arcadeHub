Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

Arcade Hub ("the app") is a collection of classic arcade games. We respect your privacy. This policy explains what data the app uses and how it is stored.

Data we collect

The app does not collect, transmit, or sell personal information. No account registration is required. We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking.

Data stored on your device

The app saves the following information locally on your device only:

  • Virtual coin balance and unlocked games
  • High scores for each game
  • Basic gameplay preferences saved by the app

This data is stored in your device's browser storage (localStorage) and is not sent to any server. Uninstalling the app removes this data.

Network access

The app works fully offline. All games and assets are bundled inside the app. No internet connection is required to play.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: arcadehub.support@example.com
