FULL DESCRIPTION
----------------
ARCADE HUB is a retro neon arcade with eleven games in one app. Pick a cabinet, chase your high score, earn coins, and unlock new titles — all offline, no login required.

NEON SIEGE
A tower defense game set in a geometric neon battlefield. Place and upgrade towers, survive enemy waves, defend the core, and push your strategy across two maps with combos and overcharge strikes.

PRISM CASCADE
A timed match puzzle built around glowing gems. Swap tiles, trigger cascading combos, and clear objectives before the clock runs out. Easy to learn, hard to put down.

THE FULL LINEUP
Space Blocks, Snake, Asteroids, Fly Hard, Space Hopper, Space Aliens, Neon 2048, Stack Tower, and Thrust Runner — arcade classics and quick-session challenges in one shared hub with animated game previews on every card.

ORIGINAL SOUNDTRACK
Browse the arcade to a rotating library of hub background music. Punchy sound effects, optional vibration feedback, and smooth audio transitions when you jump in and out of games.

FEATURES
- Eleven games in a single install
- Per-game high scores saved on your device
- Coin rewards and unlockable games
- Touch controls for phone and tablet
- Fully offline — play anywhere
- Unified neon arcade interface

One app. One hub. Your best run is always the next one.
